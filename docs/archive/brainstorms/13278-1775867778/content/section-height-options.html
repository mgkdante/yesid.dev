Section Height — How Much Room?

Same approved cards. The question is how much vertical space surrounds them.

A

Content-Fit

Just enough padding to breathe — ~80px top, ~80px bottom. The grid is the section. No forced height. Tight and efficient.

~ Section diagram
Services
~60-70% viewport height

Pros

  • Respects the "exhale" — no wasted space
  • User scrolls through quickly to next section
  • Feels purposeful and efficient

Cons

  • May feel rushed after the big dramatic sections
B

Generous Padding

Large padding — ~140px top, ~140px bottom. Grid sits in the center with room to breathe. Section might be ~85-90vh. Not forced full-viewport, but close.

~ Section diagram
Services
~85-90% viewport height

Pros

  • Cards feel important, not cramped
  • Natural visual pause in the page rhythm
  • The "exhale" landing with room to scan

Cons

  • Padding may feel empty on short viewports
C

Full Viewport (100vh)

Section is always exactly one viewport tall. Grid centered vertically within. Big dramatic frame. Consistent with hero and manifesto being full-viewport.

~ Section diagram
Services
100% viewport height

Pros

  • Each section = one "page" — clean mental model
  • Most dramatic presentation
  • Cards float in generous space

Cons

  • Too much empty space on tall monitors
  • On short viewports, cards may overflow 100vh
  • Fights the "exhale" — forced height feels rigid

My recommendation: B (generous padding). It breathes without being wasteful, and adapts naturally to any screen height. A feels too tight after dramatic sections. C risks overflow on short viewports.
