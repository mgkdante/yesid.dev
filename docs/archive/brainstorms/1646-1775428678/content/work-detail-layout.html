B. Two-Column (Sticky Sidebar)

Content sections on the left, sticky sidebar on the right with tech stack + links. Sidebar stays visible while scrolling through sections. Collapses to stacked on mobile.

My recommendation: A (Stacked) — simpler, works great on all breakpoints, and the data model (ProjectSection[]) maps directly to stacked cards with scroll-reveal. The two-column approach adds complexity for limited benefit at this content volume.
