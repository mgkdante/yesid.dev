Metro Line Strip (shown in full mockup above)

Horizontal metro line with numbered dots, short labels, and track segments connecting them. Active station glows. Feels like a real transit map — matches the site's metro identity. On mobile: collapses to just the dots (no labels).

Station Tabs (shown directly above)

Tab bar with station numbers and names. Active tab gets the orange bottom border. Cleaner, more conventional — like a real tab UI but with station numbering. Easier to read on smaller screens. On mobile: horizontally scrollable.

Interaction Behavior

Scroll behavior: Vertical scroll with snap — each service locks into viewport. The top strip updates as you scroll (active station changes via ScrollTrigger onUpdate).

Click navigation: Clicking a station in the top strip uses scrollTo to jump to that service's viewport. The SVG DrawSVGPlugin animation replays on entry regardless of direction.

Left metro line: Stays fixed on the left edge. Station dots fill/glow as you scroll past them. Already-visited stations stay filled (orange). Future ones remain hollow.

Proof strip: Always visible at the bottom. Updates per-service — shows that service's related projects as mini-cards linking to /work/[slug]. Project count on the right.

Mobile: Metro line hidden. Top strip collapses to dots-only (metro line) or scrollable tabs. SVG scales down. Proof strip stacks vertically.
