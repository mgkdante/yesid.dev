Why I Left the ORM for Raw SQL
March 15, 2026
The moment I wrote my first raw SQL query after months of ORM abstraction, something clicked. The query was faster, the intent was clearer, and I could actually see what the database was doing.
# The Problem with Abstractions
ORMs promise productivity. They deliver it — until they don't. The moment your query needs a window function, a CTE, or a lateral join, the ORM becomes a cage...
📋 Copy
SELECT route_id, stop_name,
  LAG(arrival_time) OVER (PARTITION BY trip_id)
FROM stop_times;
Try expressing that in Django ORM. You can't — not without dropping to raw() anyway...
