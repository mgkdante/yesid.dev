WHAT'S NEW IN V6

NEXT-STOP INDICATOR (TOP)

Circle-line-circle station strip across the top. Shows your journey: Sherbrooke → Lennoxville → Berri-UQAM (passed) → MANIFESTO (active, glowing) → Proof → Services → Contact (upcoming). Maps site sections as metro stops.

BECK-STYLE ROUTE LINES

Harry Beck's London Underground map rules: only horizontal, vertical, and 45° diagonals. Faint orange route segments in the background connect the corners to the roundels. Echoes the hero's metro network.

LINE ROUNDELS

Numbered circles at route line endpoints — Line 1 (orange), Line 2 (green), Line 4 (blue). STM Montreal colors. Placed where Beck lines terminate. Ghost-level opacity (20-25%).

ARRIVAL COUNTDOWN

"PROCHAIN / NEXT" with a ticking countdown timer (bottom-left). Bilingual label — French first (Montréal convention). Tabular numerals. Ticks down in real time.

DIRECTIONAL CHEVRONS

Three stacked chevrons pointing right (top-right) and down (top-left). Classic wayfinding markers from metro platforms. 12% opacity — barely there but unmistakably transit.

PLATFORM BADGES + BILINGUAL

"QUAI / PLATFORM 2" (bottom-right) and "DIRECTION: CENTRE-VILLE" (top-left). French/English bilingual. "LIGNE ORANGE" in the bottom status bar. All deeply Montréal.

EASTER EGGS

1) Next-stop strip: "SHERBROOKE" and "LENNOXVILLE" as the first two passed stations — your origin before Montréal. 2) Right edge coords: SRC "Sherbrooke, QC" / VIA "Lennoxville, QC" / DST "Montréal, QC" — your life route, hidden in the data readout.

EVERYTHING PRESERVED

Circuit grid, interactive node canvas, cursor proximity glow, trace connections, warm glow follow, tap ripples, data flow lines, construction stripes (bold), all edge decorations from v4/v5. This is additive.
