Hero + Manifesto Merge: Which Architecture?

Research says: max 2-3vh pin, one color break, no text inside pins. Which approach best respects the psychology while feeling cinematic?

A

Extended Hero (daveholloway model)

Add manifesto as phases 10-12 of the existing 9-phase hero pin. One continuous pinned section at ~1800% scroll.

Pros
Simplest, hero untouched, cohesive identity
Cons
18vh pin (research max: 2-3vh). No "break" moment. Manifesto text inside pin = worst UX combo per NNGroup
RESEARCH VERDICT: Fails pin ceiling + text-in-pin rule
B

Act Break (digitalflagship model)

Hero at 1200% (untouched). Manifesto is SEPARATE section with warm amber bg shift + SVG circuit texture. "INFRASTRUCTURE" scales viewport-width.

Pros
Creates the Von Restorff "break." Manifesto text can be massive. Brand reinforcement. Separate scroll zones.
Cons
Hero still at 12vh pin. Two separate sections = potential scroll jank at transition.
RESEARCH VERDICT: Closest to correct, but hero pin too long
C

Hybrid Crossfade (jasminegunarto model)

ONE pinned section at ~1600%. Phases 1-9: existing hero. Phase 9-10: bg transitions warm. Phase 10-12: hero scales down, manifesto scales up.

Pros
Seamless crossfade, most cinematic, "infrastructure materializing" metaphor
Cons
16vh pin (worst of all). 12+ phases = scroll fatigue. Most complex technically. Text inside pin.
RESEARCH VERDICT: Fails pin ceiling, text-in-pin, and complexity
D

Modified B: "Shortened Hero + Act Break" (my recommendation)

Shorten hero to ~800% (8vh) — pure visual metro animation, no text reading required. Then HARD CUT to manifesto on NORMAL SCROLL (not pinned) with warm amber bg shift.

Page Flow:
[ACT 1a] Hero pin ~800% — metro SVG draws, zooms into Berri, reveals "yesid." wordmark
[HARD CUT] — abrupt bg shift, no gradient
[ACT 1b] Manifesto NORMAL SCROLL — warm amber bg + SVG circuit texture
    "I BUILD THE" (small) → "INFRASTRUCTURE" (3x larger, viewport-fill) → "YOUR OPERATIONS RUN ON" (small)
    SplitText char reveal on scroll-into-view (not pinned)
    Capability pills stagger in after
[ACT 2] Proof Reel — back to dark
[BREAK] Services Grid — warm amber (reuses break color)
[ACT 3] Blog + About + CTA — dark, orange glow on CTA
Why this works
Pin ceiling: 8vh (vs 12-18vh in others) — within research guidelines
No text in pin: Hero is pure visual animation
Von Restorff break: Manifesto IS the one color shift
Variable size: "INFRASTRUCTURE" at 3x = research-optimal ratio
Normal scroll: Manifesto scrolls naturally = no frustration
Mobile safe: Pin simplified, manifesto fully responsive
Research-backed constraints
1. Pin max: 2-3 viewport heights (NNGroup)
2. No reading text inside pins (NNGroup)
3. One color break, singular (Von Restorff)
4. Break at 60-70% scroll depth (attention curve)
5. Variable size ratio: 2.5-3.5x (eye tracking)
6. Never flat dark — always textured (depth perception)
