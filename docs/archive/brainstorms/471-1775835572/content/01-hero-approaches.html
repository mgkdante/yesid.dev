My Recommendation: B (Proof Grid)

It solves the emptiness with visual richness (metric cards + tech pills woven between headline text), leads with what the visitor gains ("YOUR DATA. ENGINEERED RIGHT."), and scales as you grow — swap numbers, add client logos later. The metrics don't need clients: databases you've designed, dashboards you've shipped (including personal/learning projects), pipelines you've built. It's honest, visual, and Carnegie-approved.
