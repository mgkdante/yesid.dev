Key Takeaways for yesid.dev

What makes them feel "full"
  • Text fills 80%+ of viewport — no breathing room
  • Background is never flat — grids, gradients, scanlines, grain
  • Variable sizing creates rhythm (big + small = dense)
  • Overlays add depth layers (even subtle ones)
  • Tight line-height (0.8–0.9) packs text vertically
Applicable to our hero+manifesto merge
  • Scale "DIGITAL INFRA" to fill viewport width (clamp to ~15vw+)
  • Add SVG grid or dot-matrix pattern behind text
  • Manifesto text woven into/below hero as one flow
  • Capability pills as interactive hover-reveal elements
  • Orange glow or gradient wash for brand warmth
