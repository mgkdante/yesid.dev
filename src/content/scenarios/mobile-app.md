You want your data and services available in your users' pockets — a mobile application that connects to your backend APIs and delivers a native experience. This stack offers two paths: Kotlin with Jetpack Compose for native Android with Material Design 3, or Flutter for cross-platform iOS and Android from a single codebase. Both connect through REST APIs with typed contracts, ensuring the mobile app stays in sync with your backend as it evolves. The choice between native and cross-platform depends on your timeline, team, and platform priorities.
