# Support

For consulting or general questions, use
[yesid.dev/contact](https://yesid.dev/contact) or email `contact@yesid.dev`.

Public issues may be used for reproducible defects in the public site. Include
the URL, browser and operating system, expected result, actual result, and
reproduction steps.

Security reports must follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not a public issue.
