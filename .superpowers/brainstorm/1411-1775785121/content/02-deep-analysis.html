Three Implementation Proposals

Click to select your preferred direction

A

Extended Hero — "daveholloway approach"

Concept: Keep existing 9-phase scroll animation. Add phases 10-12 where headline scales down and manifesto text reveals inline. ONE continuous pinned section.

Implementation:
  • Extend ScrollTrigger pin from 1200% → 1800%
  • Phase 10: "DIGITAL INFRA" scales from viewport-width to top-left badge
  • Phase 11: Manifesto chars reveal (SplitText) filling the freed viewport space
  • Phase 12: Capability pills stagger in below manifesto
  • Add SVG dot-grid pattern behind everything (subtle, like digitalflagship)
  • Same dark bg throughout — cohesive single identity section
Pros:
  • Your existing animation stays untouched
  • One continuous experience — no "two empty screens" problem
  • Technically simplest (extend existing timeline)
Cons:
  • Very long pin (1800% scroll = ~17 viewport-heights of scrolling)
  • No dramatic "break" moment — all one tone
  • Manifesto text can't be as oversized (constrained by hero layout)
B

Act Break — "digitalflagship approach"

Concept: Hero ends at phase 9, unpins cleanly. Manifesto section is a SEPARATE section with different visual treatment — an orange/amber gradient wash that acts as the "break" for the whole page.

Implementation:
  • Hero stays 1200% (untouched)
  • Manifesto: new pinned section, 300% scroll
  • Background: subtle gradient from #141414 → rgba(224,120,0,0.08) (warm amber tint)
  • SVG circuit-board/dot-grid pattern overlay (brand-techy feel)
  • Text: "INFRASTRUCTURE" scales to clamp(4rem, 15vw, 14rem) — viewport-filling like byfrontyard's "be"
  • Variable sizing: "I BUILD THE" small → "INFRASTRUCTURE" massive → "YOUR OPERATIONS RUN ON." medium
  • Pills float up with stagger, each links to /services/[id]
Pros:
  • Creates the "act break" that makes Awwwards pages feel dynamic
  • Manifesto text can be HUGE (own viewport, no hero constraints)
  • Orange gradient = brand reinforcement moment
  • Rest of home page sections after manifesto feel like "Act 2"
Cons:
  • Still two separate pinned sections (potential scroll jank at transition)
  • Gradient needs careful tuning to not feel "too branded" / cheesy
C

Hybrid — "jasminegunarto approach"

Concept: ONE massive pinned section (hero + manifesto unified) BUT the background itself transitions during scroll. Dark + grid for hero phases → warm amber for manifesto phases. Best of both worlds.

Implementation:
  • Single ScrollTrigger pin, ~1600% scroll
  • Phases 1-9: existing hero animation (dark bg, metro SVG)
  • Phase 9→10 transition: bg color animates from #141414 to #1a1510 (warm dark)
  • SVG dot-grid opacity fades in during transition
  • Phase 10: hero headline scales down, manifesto text scales up simultaneously
  • Phase 11: manifesto fills viewport, "INFRASTRUCTURE" at max size
  • Phase 12: pills stagger, section unpins
  • Below: rest of home page in standard dark #141414
Pros:
  • Seamless — no scroll jank between hero and manifesto
  • Background transition creates subtle "break" within one section
  • Hero crossfades into manifesto (most cinematic)
  • SVG grid appears DURING transition = "infrastructure materializing"
Cons:
  • Most technically complex (GSAP timeline needs hero + manifesto + bg transitions)
  • Debugging scroll phases is harder with 12+ phases
  • Risk of scroll fatigue (1600% = ~15 viewports of pinned scroll)
