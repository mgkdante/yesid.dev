Adapted from the STM map — simplified into a hero-scale SVG. The orange line is the star.

Hero Animation: Network Shape (dark background)

Animation Sequence Concept

1. Single glowing orange dot at center (Berri-UQAM position)
2. Orange line draws outward in both directions — the U-shape extends
3. Station nodes pop in as the line passes through them
4. Green line draws from center interchange outward
5. Blue line draws across the top
6. Yellow stub appears last
7. Hub stations pulse — the network is alive
8. Camera zooms into the origin dot → becomes the "." in "Data Infra."
