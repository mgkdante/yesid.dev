C — Bottom Mural Strip

SVG at ~10% opacity runs along the BOTTOM edge like real station-level graffiti. Content sits above on clean wall. Fade gradient blends art into dark. Most realistic — graffiti is always at ground level. Reveals on scroll as user reaches the bottom.

Implementation Notes

SVG recoloring: CSS filter: hue-rotate() saturate() brightness() to shift reds/oranges to brand #E07800/#FFB627. Or we edit the SVG source directly (replacing rgb(238,64,54) and rgb(240,90,40) with brand values).

Performance: SVG is 409KB. We'll inline the critical paths or lazy-load with loading="lazy". Could also optimize with SVGO to reduce size.

Attribution: Required per Vecteezy license. Small text in section or footer: "Graffiti Vectors by Vecteezy" with link.

Animation: SVG fades in (opacity 0 → target) on scroll entrance via GSAP ScrollTrigger. Drips could animate downward.
